Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the French & English Matching Game website. We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how we handle information.

Data we collect

This site stores game progress and preferences only in your own browser (e.g. level, score, language, and theme). This data is not sent to our servers. We do not collect personal information such as your name, email, or address.

Cookies and local storage

We may use your browser’s local storage to save game progress and settings so you can continue where you left off. We do not use cookies for tracking. Third parties (see below) may use cookies and similar technologies when ads are shown on this site.

Third-party advertising and Google

This site may display ads provided by Google AdSense. When ads are shown, third parties (including Google) may use cookies, web beacons, or similar technologies to serve ads and measure ad performance. Data collected in this way is subject to those companies’ privacy policies.

For more information about how Google uses data when you use our partners’ sites or apps, see: How Google uses data when you use our partners’ sites or apps.

You can control ad personalization and cookie settings via Google’s Ad Settings.
