Privacy Policy

This privacy notice covers the French & English Matching Game site. Your personal details matter, so we aim to show exactly how data moves through our systems.

Data Collection and How It Is Used

Right inside your browser, game stats like level, top score, chosen language, along with display look stay put. Stuck on your machine, that info never heads off to remote computers. Your name, where you live, message hub? None of it gets asked. Collected? Kept? Nope.

Cookies and Local Storage

Inside your browser, local storage holds where you left off in the game - coming back feels smooth. Tracking or watching activity? Not done by us through first-party cookies. When ads show up here, outside helpers might rely on cookies or tech like them. These tools help things run while shaping some parts of what you see.

Third Party Ads and Google

Ads here could come through Google AdSense. If they appear, outside groups - Google included - might place cookies or use web beacons. These tools help show fitting ads, also track how well they perform. The information gathered follows each company's own privacy rules. How that data gets used depends on their individual policies, not this site. Tracking happens behind the scenes when banners load. No control exists locally over what details get pulled. Privacy choices sit with the firms running the ad network. Their tech watches visits to adjust future content. Each party applies its own standards once info flows in.

Jump over to this page if you want details about how Google handles data: How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps. Your choices matter - adjust them any time using Google's Ad Settings to step away from tailored ads.

Policy Updates

From time to time, we might adjust how this privacy policy works - either because of new laws or shifts in what we do. That change will show up right away in the date marked "Last updated" underneath.
