FAQ

This game - what could it be?

A timed challenge matches French words with their English counterparts. Cards turn over one by one looking for links like chien to dog. Each round tests memory under pressure trying to complete before the clock stops. Pairs click together when found building progress slowly. The pace stays quick keeping attention sharp throughout.

How Do I Play?

Start by picking Animals, Colors, Numbers, Family, or Verbs. Instead of rushing, wait for the chosen difficulty to load. Once cards appear, watch closely - they show only briefly. After that moment passes, select any two, one after another. Success means finding identical ones before time runs out. Victory comes only if every pair is matched on time. Stars stack up based on performance - two or more opens what follows. Next stage unlocks just when score meets the mark.

Is it free?

Fine, you can play it without paying a cent.

Who is it for?

People picking up French or just refreshing their skills might find this useful. So could those working on English. It suits students, educators, even someone practicing now and then. Switching between English and Français takes one click. The whole layout adjusts smoothly.

Ways to Support the Game?
